Its goal is to provide a lightweight solution that can be easily implemented in any development context.

 Simple and focused

With simple class names like .lx-btn or .lx-row, and a simple modifier system, like .has-dflex .

 Free your creative

You don't need to take a course to use it, with no distractions to interrupt your creative flow.

 Stay independent

Luxa CSS is totally free. Just do what you want, seriously!
